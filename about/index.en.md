**Welcome to the Tegaki Tuesday ([手]{て}[書]{が}きの[火]{か}[曜]{よう}[日]{び}) website!**

Tegaki Tuesday is a weekly Japanese/English handwriting challenge that happens every, you guessed it, Tuesday. For each challenge, a short passage is selected, usually from the lyrics of a song. Then, it's your job to handwrite (either physically or on a graphics tablet) the challenge and submit it! New challenges are posted weekly on Tuesday at **11:59 PM PST.**

Done with this week's challenge? You don't have to wait a whole week! Feel free to browse through our backlog [hundreds of past challenges](/challenges) to write and submit if you want to get extra practice. Nothing interests you? Add it to the site yourself! [Create a community challenge.](/new)

Looking for something quick while waiting for the next Tegaki Tuesday? We have a [Kanji of the Day](/kotd/today) (KOTD) challenge! A random [Jōyō](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Jōyō_kanji) kanji is picked every 24 hours at **12:00 AM PST.** When you're done, feel free to go back and submit previous days. (Or even kanji that haven't been KOTD yet!)

In the **#handwriting** channel of a participating server, use the `/submit` slash command with an image attachment to submit. [字]{じ}ちゃん (Ji-chan) will automatically upload your submission to the website for you! You can submit as many times as you want.

## 字ちゃん

Want to use 字ちゃん's utility commands on your own server? [Add 字ちゃん to your own server!](https://discord.com/oauth2/authorize?client_id=819008332609880076) Keep in mind that challenge interaction is limited to officially partnered servers.

Most commands have both prefix (e.g. `-h submit`) and slash command (e.g. `/submit`) variants.

- `submit <image> [challenge]` \
  Submit to the latest handwriting challenge, or a specific one by number, kanji, or slug.

  Using the prefix version, `-h submit [challenge]`, you can also reply to a submission embed (to re-tag it), or to a message with an image (to add it), in addition to normal usage. This applies to `-h submitkotd` as well.
- `submitkotd <image>` Submit to today's Kanji of the Day.
- `challenge` View the latest handwriting challenge info.
- `suggest` Suggest a song or poem for a future challenge. Opens a form, so it's available as a slash command only.
- `i <text>` Get category information and links to Jisho for character(s), along with a stroke order diagram for single characters.
- `so <text>` Get stroke order diagrams for character(s), maximum 4.
- `jinmeiyo` Random [Jinmeiyō](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Jinmeiyō_kanji) kanji.
- `joyo` Random [Jōyō](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Jōyō_kanji) kanji.
- `kyoiku [grade|ALL]` Random [Kyōiku](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kyōiku_kanji) kanji.
- `jlpt [level|ALL]` Random [<abbr title="Japanese-Language Proficiency Test">JLPT</abbr>](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Japanese-Language_Proficiency_Test) kanji.
- `hyogai [group|ALL]` Random [Hyōgai](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hyōgai_kanji) kanji.
- `help` Show 字ちゃん's command help menu.
